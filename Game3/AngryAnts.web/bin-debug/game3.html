AntTracker Game




Having trouble loading or playing the game? Consult the FAQ.
Play Instructions:
  • The ant to be tracked is circled in red.
  • To progress the video on the foremost frame (signified by a disabled right arrow button), aim for the midsection of the ant and click.
  • After any click on the video, the video will play for the next second during which clicking is disabled.
  • Jump-and-seek clicks in previous frames by clicking the navigation arrow buttons or pushing the keyboard arrows.
  • The most recent click may be undone on the second foremost frame through the right-click menu or with the key combination 'Ctrl-Z'.
  • The playback speed slider can be adjusted at any time during the game.
  • Toggle the show path button for a visual overlay of the linked clicks so far.
